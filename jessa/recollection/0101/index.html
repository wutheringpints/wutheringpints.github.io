PART 1: WHAT IS YOUR NAME?

< -- 985-04-12, 13:32. DISTRICT 8 (H CORP). -- > >

A "young" lady stands by the entrance of the Hongyuan Warp Station. I say "young" with a hint of irony, as the first thing you are to know about her is that though she looks about twenty, it's quite difficult to decipher her actual age. It could in fact be twenty, or it could be higher. either way, it is in no way important to the story, so I will continue.


It is on this day, 985-04-12 that this young lady was given a very important job within District 8. As she walks through the boxy and claustrophobic nature of this particular area, and smelling what could only be described as the smell of a gas station, with its sweet and strong odour, she recalls the job she was written up to do prior to entering this part of the City. There's a certain rather high ranking officer of the one of The Fingers hiding within this area, A Big Brother of the Middle. known by locals as "Shark" due to his incredibly sharp teeth, and eagerness to write anyone who dares even look at him slightly wrong. In short, He is incredibly petty. I'll spare you the details of his appearance for now, as we ought to talk about the reason the Lady was specifically called up to this particular job.


As the sun sets (though not particularly important in this area, as sunlight is sort of a foreign concept to its artificially lit surfaces) and the Lady walks to her destination, appreciating the way the streetlights dim mimicking the sun in its absence, and the sound of the working man seems to hush itself (though only by a small margin), I will begin to tell you the second thing to know about our Lady. Though it may come as a surprise to you, this particular woman was a babysitter in the past as part of a maid job she had in T corp. This is why she was sought out for this specific job, as she has a sort of vendetta against people like our Shark. You see recently this particular Big Brother destroyed a family of four over a small incident regarding food. The incident itself doesn't matter all too much, what matters is this family has been destroyed by a man as pathetic and horrible as Shark, which absolutely will not fly with our friend the Lady. The Lady can't stand people like Shark, they are entirely beneath her as disgusting rats, scurrying around in the pantry of her ladies manor. Today one of those rats (or, in a more forward manner, Shark) will meet a fittingly pathetic death.


I believe this is enough description on where we are for now, so I shall let her do the talking for a moment.
